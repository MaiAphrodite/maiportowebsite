# Dialogue Sound Effects

Place your sound files in this directory.

## Naming Convention
The system expects files in this format:
`bleepXXX.ogg` 
(where XXX is the number, e.g., 001, 015, 030)

## Categories
The system expects 30 files mapped as follows:

- **Mid Tone (001 - 010)**
  - Normal speech
  - Files: `bleep001.ogg` to `bleep010.ogg`

- **Low Tone (011 - 020)**
  - Used for periods (.), long words, or serious tone
  - Files: `bleep011.ogg` to `bleep020.ogg`

- **High Tone (021 - 030)**
  - Used for questions (?), exclamations (!), or excitement
  - Files: `bleep021.ogg` to `bleep030.ogg`

## Example
- `bleep001.ogg`
- `bleep015.ogg`
- `bleep030.ogg`
